About Me

I am Miss Candy. I started baking in highschool. At that my period I usually went to my bestfriends houses with any kind of cake I baked that morning. And since... well I just strated then, lets say that they were my beta-tester... Of course, I did not bake every week for my friends. We saw each other plenty of time at school, and there was other things to do other than just see each other (*cough* Homeworks. *cough* Tests.). But if you are lucky, like me, you have at home an adept of pastry. Who'll eat anything you bake, for the sole reason it is cake, I name my twin sister. We were a great team: I'd bake the pastries... She'd eat them!

Anyway, thank you for exploring my website! As you can see I still struggle a bit with coding but I really enjoy doing it!
Let me welcome you to my Candyland! the result of my six years of home bakering!
